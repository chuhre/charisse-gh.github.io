Introduction to Peach Riot

What is Peach Riot?

Peach Riot is a Pop Mart figurine series created by Libby Frame. They are a fictional high school punk band which is formed by 3 memebers - Gigi, Frankie, and Poppy. They release music that you can listen to on Spotify, Youtube and Apple Music.

History of Peach Riot

Peach Riot was created by Libby Frame.

During her academic years, she minored in film studies and majored in illustration design. She started working as a freelance artist after graduating, specializing in conceptual art and character design and frequently incorporating narrative into her works.

Joining POPMART in 2024, these characters have quickly become a fan favorite! The grunge movement and the riot girl band movement served as inspiration for Peach Riot. They are all American and take place in the Pacific Northwest in the 1990s. Additionally, they were influenced by the anime Fooly Cooly (FLCL), which battled with guitars. They are supposed to evoke music, pleasure, and youth! They manage to be both nostalgic and up to date at the same time. The finest aspects of both worlds are reimagined.
